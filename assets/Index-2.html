Welcome to SENF Group -MDECCE (Maharshi Dayanand childhood care & education)
 

Maharshi Dayanand Childhood Care & EducationTM is a Unit of ' Shiv Educational & Foundation Group. 

Shiv Educational Yoga SansthanTM  is a unit of Shiv Educational & Foundation Group (SENF Group)TM

MDECCE  & Shiv Educational Yoga Sansthan are the member of QCI .Under QCI Monitoring Mem. no. is Approved.

After completing one year yoga Diploma ,Yoga Certification QCI Exam is necessary for Govt. Job.

 
 

tSENF Group' (Shiv Educational & Foundation Group), an educational organization started various Job Oriented Courses, short term computer courses, short term vocational courses, AANGANWARI, etc. with the help & cooperation of educational experts with view to train unemployed educated students, particularly the candidates of Scheduled Cast, Scheduled Tribes and backwards so that they may impart education based on modern lines with a stress on moral, intellectual, physical and social personality. This organization takes guidance from N.C.E.R.T., New Delhi.

Affiliation of yoga, computer course, vocational course,NTT/PTT,Teacher training courses , application form are invited
